Why can't you link to that image?

If you're here it's probably because you saw this nasty graphic on a page:

Odd's are that it's not directly your fault, so I wouldn't be too concerned. But here's a bit of an explanation.

This site is run as a private sort of hobby for myself, my wife, and a few other family members. Being as I'm broke, I just run it off my comcast cable modem. Occassionaly someone on a higher traffic message board will find a picture of my site and link to it in a posting. This causes many, many people to view this image, using my bandwidth, slowing my own connection, and does not let the viewer know the context or the source of the image. As far as web-etiquette goes, this is considered to be very rude and also doesn't follow the BY-NC-SA license that I have on my works. So, to conserve my bandwidth, I've resorted to checking referers.

If you got this graphic, then your referer wasn't allowed. But don't fear. If its a legitimate site, email me and I'll add your site to list of sites that hotlink to images. Here's the current list of sites that can hotlink (may be slightly out of date):
