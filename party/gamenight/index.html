Game Night

Would you like to play a game of chess?

Okay, so chess is a fun game, but it's not exactly the most exciting of games. And I'm sure that most people are so sick of "party" games like Taboo and Cranium that they wish they'd never played them. Well never fear, we ensure you that this once in a period-of-time event will be 100% Taboo and Cranium free.

Here's the deal. We've got lots of interesting, geeky, and fun games that we don't get play enough. The solution, invite a bunch of fun and interesting folks over to play our games. So come on over and experience something new.

The Lowdown

Patrick and Kristina's Game Night
Friday, March 11th, 2005
Starting around 6ish (If you'd like to come earlier, we can do a dinner thing too)
436 44th St directions, map
412-606-9730 (Patrick)

If you've got an interesting game you'd like to bring, let us know and I'll add it to the list, as long as it isn't Cranium or Taboo. Seriously, no Cranium or Taboo. I'm not joking about this.

We'll provide some munchies, but like all events like this, if you'd like to bring something along to munch on, you're more than welcome.
